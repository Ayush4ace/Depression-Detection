Machine Learning-based Depression Detection using Video Analysis

Machine Learning-based Depression Detection using Video Analysis is to develop an accurate, non-invasive, and cost-effective method of detecting depression in individuals. The traditional approach to depression diagnosis relies on subjective assessments by mental health professionals and self-reporting by patients, which can be prone to errors and biases. Video analysis allows for the detection of patterns and markers in an individual's facial expressions and other visual cues that are unique to individuals with depression. Machine learning algorithms can be trained on large datasets of video footage to identify these patterns and markers, which can then be used to predict.
